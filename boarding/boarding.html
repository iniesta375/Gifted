Boarding Procedures

Pickup and Drop Off Times

Dogs scheduled to go home, **must be picked up** between the hours of **8:00AM–12:00PM on Monday–Saturday**. If dogs are not picked up by 12:00PM there will be a **$20 late pickup charge** assessed for the 12:00–5:00PM slot. If the dogs are picked up between 5:00–7:00PM that night, an additional **$20.00** late fee will be added to the daycare fee.

Drop Offs

Boarding dogs arriving have a drop off time between the hours of **12:00–5:00PM Monday–Friday** and **Saturday 12:00–3:00PM**. If your dog needs to be dropped off before 12PM there will be an **early $20 check-in fee**.

Sunday Schedule

Pickups available from **08:00AM – 12:00PM** for **additional $20 fee**. No drop offs.

Boarding disclaimer for pickups from 5pm – 7pm: Prior arrangements must be made with the front desk and pre-payment must be made.
